Thailand 2019



March 25, Bangkok



We spent our first day in Thailand exploring Bangkok. We got lunch and mango sticky rice then walked to Lumphini Park.







We took a Grab to an outdoor mall called The Riverfront where we stopped for pictures and snacks.







The best coconut ice cream of the trip!



For both nights we spent in Bangkok, we had dinner and dessert at the Train Night Market.







My favorite dessert from the night market was Roti. I liked it best with bananas, egg, and cheese inside and nutella on top.



The view from our hotel.

March 27, Khao Yai National Park



We drove about 2 hours out of Bangkok to go on a safari in Khao Yai National Park.



Lunch, snacks, and leech protection socks were provided!

March 28, Phuket



Fruit Smoothies!



We took an Island Hopping Tour to a few different islands off Phuket.







At several islands we got off the boat and went canoeing through small caves into the middle of the islands.







The guides baited monkeys into the water with bananas to entertain us.


James Bond Island





March 30, Phuket





ATVing and Ziplining



We ATVed up to the Big Buddha Statue.



Our favorite mistranslation



We spent the afternoon snorkeling, swimming, relaxing under the palm trees, and drinking more fruit smoothies from stands along the path leading to the beach.



March 31, Phuket



We spent the entire morning at Merlin Beach sitting in the water and talking. It was so beautiful and peaceful but we all left very sunburnt!







On our last night in Phuket a local woman taught us how to cook Thai food! She took us shopping at her local market and then taught us how to make green curry, pad thai, tom yum soup, and spring rolls.









The view from our hotel room in Phuket.
